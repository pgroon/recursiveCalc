Right now, this works in the console only.

You can call any of the following functions:

calc.collatz(n): Will give you the Collatz-Sequence of n.
calc.factorial(n): Will calculate n!, that is, the factorial of n.
calc.baseConv(n, base, []): Will convert n to a number of whatever base you specified. The third argument needs to be an empty array, because changing this just isn't worth the effort for now.
